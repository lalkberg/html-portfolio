The Best Films of all Time in Leo's Opinion

My top 4 films

Little Women

Beautiful pocket of history through the eyes of four sisters during the American Civil War. Heartbreaking and warming at the same time.

Back to the Future

Insanely entertaining sci-fi comedy about a kid from the 80's who's traveled back to the 1950's, desperately trying to get home while making sure his parents fall in love.

Paris, Texas

The most beautiful movie ever put to film. Follows a vagrant who turns up in the middle of the desert after being missing for four years, and his quest to reunite with his family.

Blade Runner

The greatest film ever made, according to it's director and I can't help but agree. In the futuristic year of 2019, a hardened former detective is tasked with "retiring" four rogue replicants, humanoid robots who are trying to escape captivity and seek eternal life at any cost.
